How We Started

Our Story

KhanNet was founded with a simple goal: give Magura residents the same high-speed internet that major cities enjoy. For too long, our community relied on expensive, unreliable connections that couldn't keep up with the demands of modern life — online education, remote work, video calls, and streaming.

We partnered with Link3 Technologies — one of Bangladesh's leading licensed ISPs — to bring national-grade fiber infrastructure to our local streets. Today, we serve hundreds of families and businesses across Shibrampur, Doarpar, and Atharkhada.

We live here. We know your streets, your names, and your connectivity frustrations. That personal connection drives everything we do — from our same-day support calls to our transparent pricing with no small-print surprises.
