Self-Help

Quick Troubleshooting Tips

Try these steps before calling us — they fix most common issues in under 5 minutes.

🔄 No Internet Connection

  1. Check that all cables are firmly plugged in
  2. Power off your router, wait 30 seconds, power back on
  3. Wait 2 minutes for the connection to re-establish
  4. Check if the router's "Internet" or "WAN" indicator light is on
  5. If still no connection, restart your computer or phone

🐌 Slow Speed

  1. Run a speed test at fast.com or speedtest.net
  2. Move closer to your WiFi router
  3. Disconnect other devices to reduce load
  4. Restart the router (see above)
  5. Check for background downloads/updates on your devices

📶 WiFi Signal Weak

  • Place the router in a central, elevated location
  • Keep it away from walls, microwaves, and thick concrete
  • Avoid placing it inside cabinets
  • Consider a WiFi extender for large homes
  • Use 5 GHz band for faster speeds at short range

💻 One Device Not Connecting

  • Turn device WiFi off, wait 10 seconds, turn back on
  • Forget the WiFi network and reconnect with the password
  • Restart the device completely
  • Check that Airplane Mode is off
  • Ensure your device's date & time is correct

🔁 Connection Keeps Dropping

  • Check that all cable connectors are dry and clean
  • Inspect for any cable damage or sharp bends
  • Move the router away from other electronics
  • Update your router's firmware if possible
  • Note the time drops occur and report to us

🌐 Specific Website Not Loading

  • Try the site on another device to isolate the issue
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser
  • Try dns.google (8.8.8.8) as your DNS if possible
  • Check if the site is globally down at downforeveryoneorjustme.com
